Life Abroad Course

For studying — and living — somewhere new.

Language is only part of the problem.

A structured English + life-skills course for international students and anyone preparing to relocate, study, work, or function abroad.

This is less about “speaking better” in general and more about handling the actual scenes of life abroad: university admin, housing, appointments, forms, classmates, coworkers, doctors, mistakes, loneliness, and asking for help.
