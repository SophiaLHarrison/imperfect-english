1–1 Classes

English that sounds more like you.

Private classes for adults who want more fluency, confidence, and ease — without putting on a textbook personality.

We work with the English you actually need to use: conversation, vocabulary, pronunciation, cultural nuance, telling stories, asking for what you need, and speaking about your life or work more naturally.

Good for adults who already have some English and want to feel less stiff, less translated, and more like themselves when they speak.

Life Abroad

For building a life somewhere unfamiliar.

A practical course for the strange little situations traditional language classes often skip.

For people preparing to live, work, study, or function more confidently in another country — especially when language is only part of the problem.

We look at the real-life stuff: appointments, housing, healthcare, bureaucracy, work communication, social life, culture shock, asking for help, and staying human when everything feels harder than it should.
