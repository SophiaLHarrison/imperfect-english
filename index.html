These lessons
are for you if...

You already have some English, but you want to feel more natural, confident, and prepared for real life.

These classes are especially useful if you're preparing to study abroad, moving to an English-speaking country, joining an international school or university, nervous about speaking with native or fluent speakers, tired of textbook English, or able to understand English but slower when speaking.

This is not mainly exam cramming or beginner grammar from zero. It is for students who want to move from “I know English” to “I can actually use English.”

Hi, I'm Sophia.

I'm a private English teacher with international teaching experience. I help students move from classroom English to real-world communication through conversation, culture, and personalized support.

My teaching style is warm, practical, direct, and flexible. I care about helping students sound natural, understand context, and feel less frozen when English stops being neat and predictable.

You do not have to be perfect here. You just have to be willing to practice.
